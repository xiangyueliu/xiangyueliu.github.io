Xiangyue Liu 刘襄阅

I am currently a visiting student supervised by Prof. Li Yi and Prof. Yang Gao at Tsinghua University. Before that, I completed my Master's from Beihang University.

My current research focus is on neural dense 3D/4D reconstruction from RGB videos, which can be utilized in content creation for AR/VR. Early work was on MVS and vSLAM, as they are fundamental to 3D and Robotics Vision.
