Xiangyue Liu (刘襄阅)

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Ping Tan. Before that, I was a visiting student (RA) at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Li Yi and Prof. Yang Gao. Previously, I got my master degree from Beihang University.

My current research interests are neural 3D/4D reconstruction, generation, and editing. Early interests were multi-view stereo (MVS) and visual simultaneous localization and mapping (visual SLAM).
