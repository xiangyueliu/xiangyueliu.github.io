Xiangyue Liu 刘襄阅

I am currently a visiting student supervised by Prof. Li Yi and Prof. Yang Gao at Tsinghua University. Before that, I've completed my Masters from Beihang University and Bachelors from Northeast Normal University.

My research interests lie in the scope of computer vision and computer graphics. More specifically, my current focus is on Neural Implicit Surface Reconstruction, and early work was on MVS (Multi-view Reconstruction) and vSLAM (visual Simultaneous Localization and Mapping), as they are fundamental to 3D and Robotics Vision.
