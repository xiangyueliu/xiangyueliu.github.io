Bio

I am a visiting student at Tsinghua University, IIIS now. Previously, I finished my Master of Engineering at Beihang University (985 Project University) in Jun 2021. My research interest lies in 3D vision, including neural scene representation, multi-view reconstruction, SLAM, AR&VR.
